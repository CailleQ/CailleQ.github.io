Company Profile

Engineering Excellence: Aoke Industries ApS

Aoke Industries ApS, established in 2006, is located in the Aarhus Danmarke. The company primarily engages in the precision processing of steel plates and the manufacturing of equipment structural components, providing structural components and supporting services to world-class manufacturers in sectors such as new energy equipment, construction machinery, and lifting machinery. Its major clients include globally renowned enterprises like VESTAS, CATERPILLAR, GEITH, KONE, MONTABERT, WACKER NEUSON, and SANY.

Growth and Technological Advancement

Over 15 years of development, the company has grown into a distinguished manufacturer specializing in equipment structural components, with a facility spanning 45,000 square meters, an annual sales volume of approximately 450 million RMB, and a workforce of over 300 employees.

Aoke Industries ApS boasts an array of advanced production and testing equipment, including laser/gas cutting machines, CNC bending machines, beveling robots, welding robots, large CNC boring/milling machining centers, coating production lines, and three-coordinate measuring machines.

Strategic Management and Operational Excellence

Managed by professional managers, the company incorporates advanced management concepts and mechanisms such as Lean production and 6 Sigma, utilizes ERP/MES systems to manage production operations, and fosters a dignified and efficient work environment for its employees and partners, aiming to create and enjoy high-quality work outcomes!
